Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Services

Human-in-the-Loop at BPO Nextdoor is a full-cycle service for AI-driven operations: validation, correction, retraining, quality assurance, and hybrid workflows that combine the strengths of people and algorithms

Request a Proposal

Cases

AI Data Validation & Quality Control for a Global Media Analytics Platform

BPO Nextdoor supports an international media intelligence provider with daily HITL validation of AI-generated classifications across Germany, Austria, and Switzerland. Our team reviews topic labels, sentiment scores, relevance ranking, and entity recognition produced by the client’s models. Thanks to human feedback loops, accuracy improved by over 35%, enabling the platform to scale multilingual coverage without compromising quality.

Hybrid AI + Human Workflow for Social Media Content Recognition

For a Berlin-based PR analytics technology startup, we designed a hybrid workflow where AI handles first-level detection of brand mentions, while our HITL specialists validate and correct ambiguous or sensitive items. This approach ensures precise categorization, removes false positives, and significantly enhances client trust in automated monitoring. The workflow flexibly adapts to campaign-specific spikes of +200–300% content volume.

Manual Validation & Correction

  • Correction of ambiguous or low-confidence items
  • Ensuring compliance with client-defined rules, taxonomies, and standards

    • AI Model Training & Continuous Improvement

  • Benchmarking model performance

  • Maintaining business continuity in automated environments

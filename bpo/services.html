Cooperation Models

Project-based cooperation (PbC)

PbC is the best match if requirements are clearly defined and well suited for pilot projects. Usually billed at an agreed-upon fixed price or an all-inclusive hourly rate.

Dedicated team (DT)

DT is the best match if you wish to have a team working according to your requirements but don’t want to spend time on its internal organizational issues. The standard DT pricing is a fixed monthly, per full-time employee fee (other arrangements possible).

Build-Operate-Transfer (BOT)

BOT is right for you if you plan to have your own legal entity in the future but would like to start quickly, cost-efficiently and risk-free with a competent business process outsourcing partner while maintaining a buy-out option.

Here’s how it works

01

BECOMING ACQUAINTED

The discussion of tasks, requirements and solutions

Contact us and we will organize an online/offline meeting. We will discuss your needs, tasks, requirements and offer you the most economically convenient services and business process outsourcing solutions.

We will review cooperation models, and adapt them as closely as possible to your business functions, needs and wishes.

02

LAUNCHING

We sign a contract and appoint a project manager

After agreeing on the details – we sign an agreement contract to launch the project in the pilot phase. We appoint a qualified coordinator responsible for launching the project.

03

RAMP-UP & TRAINING

Team and Training

The HR department selects candidates, conducts tests, and forms a team of qualified personnel in accordance with mutually agreed conditions.

The training can be carried out either by our team or by your department.

04

GO LIVE

The team starts to work in live phase

The team begins to perform specific tasks. KPIs are actively measured in combination with the Learning Curve and QA performance.

05

GROWTH

Expansion of cooperation

We readily welcome new challenges as well as the scaling up of cooperation.

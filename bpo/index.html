BPO Nextdoor — Trusted European Outsourcing Partner

BPO Nextdoor is a trusted European business process outsourcing company delivering professional BPO services for companies worldwide. We specialize in customer support outsourcing, AI data annotation, media monitoring, data management, HR and recruitment, back-office outsourcing, and Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) services for international businesses.

Our outsourcing solutions help companies reduce operational costs, improve productivity, and scale business operations efficiently. With multilingual support in 15 European languages and offices in Ukraine and Poland, BPO Nextdoor provides flexible and scalable services tailored to modern business needs.

We work with companies across industries including AI technology, retail, healthcare, finance, media, telecommunications, and energy. Our experienced teams combine advanced technologies, secure workflows, and human expertise to deliver high-quality outsourcing services for clients in the EU, UK, USA, and global markets. Whether you need customer support services, data processing, AI automation support, recruitment outsourcing, or operational assistance, BPO

Nextdoor is your reliable outsourcing partner for long-term business growth.

What we do

BPO Nextdoor is a Ukrainian company with nearly 15 years of experience working with clients across Europe and the United States.

Founded in 2012 in Lviv as a sister company of Edvantis, we have always focused on combining technology with high-quality business processes, strong language expertise, and a deep understanding of international markets.

From the very beginning, our core projects were English- and German-speaking

Where accuracy, reliability, and cultural awareness were essential. Over time, we built strong expertise in media monitoring and large-scale data processing, delivering multilingual services in 24/7 operational environments. These capabilities became the foundation of long-term partnerships with clients in the DACH region, the UK, and the US

Throughout our journey, BPO Nextdoor has gone through phases of growth

Transformation, and global challenges — while maintaining team stability and uninterrupted client operations. Today, we work at the intersection of human expertise and technology, developing analytical, linguistic, and AI-supported workflows, including Human-in-the-Loop approaches.

We are building a company where professionalism, trust, and continuous learning matter.

Joining BPO Nextdoor means becoming part of an international environment where people grow together with the projects they work on.

Our mission

We empower partners to succeed with quality service, trust, and authentic relationships, while unlocking Ukrainian talent potential.

One of the partners of VRO Mekhidoog is a German product IT company that specializes in the classification and management of data on products from the fields of electronics, engineering and mechanical engineering.

We are expanding our cooperation with it and are looking for an industrial product analyst with knowledge of German to join our team :) The main work is to search and analyze relevant information about the product (proper name, article number, manufacturer's name, etc.) on the Internet. The purpose of the search and analysis is the classification and description of the product according to the international standard ESI.A55. Most of the products relate to the fields of mechanical engineering, engineering, electronics.

During the probationary period, you will be able to undergo training with a mentor, which is necessary for successful work. And when you are selected for this position, you will have the opportunity to get acquainted with the working environment and the team in the office

Our Journey

2025 and Beyond – Trust, Innovation, Growth

With 250+ professionals, BPO Nextdoor continues to expand across Europe, combining human expertise with innovation — building trust, empowering people, and connecting Ukrainian talent with the global market.

2023–2024 – Innovation and Expertise

We joined AMEC and FIBEP and expanded into AI-driven services: Human-in-the-Loop automation, multilingual data annotation, and LLM development with Edvantis. Our upskilling programs enabled colleagues to move from routine tasks to analytical and AI-supported roles.

2020–2022 – Resilience

Despite the pandemic and war, we maintained operations, protected jobs, and strengthened client trust — proving unity and reliability in crisis.

2019 – Leadership and Transformation

Jurij Pidsadnyj became CEO, introducing a people-centric leadership philosophy based on trust, openness, and long-term partnerships.

2016–2018 – Growth and Specialization

We expanded into classic BPO services (customer support, data processing, e-commerce) and entered the media monitoring industry with KANTAR Media - laying the groundwork for one of our core service lines.

2012–2015 – Foundation

Under CEO Serhiy Sumnikov, a graduate of the University of Bonn, the company focused on German-speaking clients and linguistic precision. Our motto: “Promise less, deliver more.”

2012 – The Beginning

BPO Nextdoor was founded in Lviv as a sister company of Edvantis to combine technology with high-quality business processes.

Mission

We help partners achieve success through quality service, trust, and human connection, while creating opportunities for Ukrainian talent.

Our vision

By 2031, BPO Nextdoor will be a trusted partner for international companies, helping them efficiently manage business processes through the expertise of its teams, proprietary solutions, and a strong talent development system

BPO Nextdoor — your trusted European outsourcing company

BPO Nextdoor is a European business process outsourcing company delivering professional BPO services to clients across Europe and North America. With offices in Ukraine and Poland, we help businesses improve operational efficiency through multilingual customer support, media monitoring, data processing, recruitment support, back-office operations, and AI-assisted services.

For more than 10 years, our teams have supported international companies with scalable outsourcing solutions tailored to different industries and business models. We combine operational excellence, ISO-certified processes, and flexible cooperation models to help organizations reduce costs, increase productivity, and accelerate growth.

Our expertise includes multilingual data annotation, Human-in-the-Loop services, customer support outsourcing, e-commerce operations, and business process management. By combining experienced professionals with modern technologies and AI-supported workflows, BPO Nextdoor delivers secure, reliable, and adaptable outsourcing services for modern businesses.

We help companies optimize business processes, reduce costs, and scale with next-generation outsourcing solutions.

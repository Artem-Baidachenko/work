Ukraine signed a memorandum with the German company Diehl Defence, the manufacturer of the IRIS-T air defense system, which provides for a tripling of supplies of missiles and air defense systems. Protecting Ukraine's skies and cities is our priority. The signed agreement paves the way for major industrial projects that will strengthen Ukraine's air defense capabilities.

The memorandum also provides for a tripling of supplies of missiles and air defense systems.

  • In early September 2024, German Chancellor Olaf Scholz announced that Germany had ordered a total of 17 IRIS-T air defense systems for Ukraine. These include eight medium-range (SLM) systems and nine short-range (SLS) systems.
  • On December 20, President Volodymyr Zelenskyy confirmed that Ukraine had received its sixth IRIS-T SLM air defense system from Germany.
  • In late December 2024, Germany delivered a large aid package to Ukraine, including IRIS-T and Patriot systems.
  • According to media reports, Germany is preparing to deliver significant military aid packages to Ukraine in the first half of 2025, including Leopard tanks, Marder infantry fighting vehicles, and other weapons.
